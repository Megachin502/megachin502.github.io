English: Exterior Lamps — any electrically powered light-generating component for Exterior lighting, in the technical use of the term, such as light bulbs.
  • Some Exterior lighting subcategories for outdoor Lighting fixtures use "lamp" in their title, and are found in Category: Exterior lighting fixtures.
  • For clarity, this category and its subcategories below are only used for images of the actual electrical light source; images of exterior lighting fixtures should be placed in Category: Exterior lighting fixtures or its subcategories.
